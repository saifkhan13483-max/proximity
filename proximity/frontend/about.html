Our Story

We Fight So You
Don't Have To

Proximity was built on one simple belief: every American deserves access to accurate credit reporting and the financial opportunities it unlocks.

Our Mission

Built for Those Who've Been Left Behind by the System

Proximity was founded after our team witnessed firsthand how inaccurate credit reporting destroys financial opportunities for millions of Americans every year. We saw families denied mortgages, entrepreneurs blocked from business loans, and individuals trapped in cycles of high-interest debt — all because of errors they didn't cause and couldn't fix alone.

We built Proximity to level the playing field. Armed with deep knowledge of consumer credit law, cutting-edge dispute strategies, and a genuine commitment to our clients' success, we fight the battles that matter most.

Today, Proximity serves clients across all 50 states, with a team of dedicated credit specialists, financial coaches, and legal experts working every day to help people reclaim their financial futures.
