Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Proximity Credit Repair ("Proximity," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, use our services, or contact us. This information may include:

Personal Identification Information: Full name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.

Credit and Financial Information: Information about your credit report items, creditors, account numbers, and dispute details that you provide to us for the purpose of filing disputes on your behalf. We only collect the information you explicitly submit through our platform.

Account Information: Username, password (stored as a cryptographic hash — we never store or see your raw password), and account preferences.

Communication Data: Messages you send through our contact form and any correspondence with our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery: To file credit disputes on your behalf with Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion, to monitor the status of your disputes, and to communicate updates about your account.

Account Management: To create and manage your Proximity account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support.

Communications: To send you account notifications, dispute status updates, and important service announcements. We may also send you educational credit tips if you have opted in. You may opt out at any time.

Improvement of Services: To analyze usage patterns, fix technical issues, and improve the functionality and security of our platform. This analysis is performed on anonymized or aggregated data wherever possible.

3. How We Protect Your Data

Protecting your data is our highest priority. We implement multiple layers of security:

Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS). Sensitive data stored in our database is encrypted at rest.

Password Hashing: Your password is never stored in plain text. We use the bcrypt hashing algorithm with a cost factor of 12 — among the highest security standards in the industry.

Authentication Tokens: We use signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for session management. These tokens are stored locally on your device and are cryptographically verified on every request.

Access Controls: Only authorized Proximity personnel with a legitimate business need can access client data, and all access is logged.

Input Sanitization: All data entering our system is sanitized to prevent SQL injection, cross-site scripting (XSS), and MongoDB operator injection attacks.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties under any circumstances. We may share your information only in the following limited situations:

Credit Bureaus: We share relevant dispute information with Equifax, Experian, and/or TransUnion on your explicit behalf and with your authorization as part of the dispute process.

Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers (such as email delivery services) who assist us in operating our platform. These providers are contractually bound to keep your information confidential and may not use it for any other purpose.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as a court order or subpoena.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

Access: You may request a copy of all personal information we hold about you.

Correction: You may update or correct inaccurate information through your account dashboard or by contacting us.

Deletion: You may request that we delete your account and all associated personal data. Upon deletion, we will remove all identifiable information from our active systems within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Portability: You may request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@proximity.com.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Proximity does not use traditional browser cookies for authentication. Instead, we use the browser's localStorage to store your authentication token securely. This token is used solely to maintain your login session and is automatically cleared when you log out.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics scripts that share your data with outside parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Proximity is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us
