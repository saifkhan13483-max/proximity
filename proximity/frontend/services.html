The Process

How We Work

Our proven 5-step process delivers consistent, measurable results for every client.

1

Sign up and connect your credit report

Create your Proximity account and securely connect your credit reports from all three bureaus for a comprehensive review.

2

We analyze all 3 bureaus and identify disputable items

Our specialists review every item on your reports and identify inaccuracies, outdated information, and legally challengeable entries.

3

We draft and send certified dispute letters

Custom dispute letters are crafted for each item and sent directly to the bureaus and creditors with supporting documentation.

4

Bureaus have 30 days to investigate and respond

Under the FCRA, bureaus must investigate and respond to every valid dispute within 30 days. We monitor all responses.

5

Resolved items are removed — we track and repeat as needed

Successfully disputed items are removed from your report. For any rejections, we refine our strategy and re-dispute immediately.
