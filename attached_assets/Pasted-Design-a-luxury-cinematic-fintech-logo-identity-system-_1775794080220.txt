Design a luxury, cinematic fintech logo identity system for a brand named "PROXIMITY".

Core Design Direction:
Create a sleek, modern monogram based on the letter "P" — geometric, precise, and subtly unique. The mark should feel like it belongs to an elite wealth management firm or premium financial institution. No mascots, no clutter, no complexity. Minimal, timeless, powerful.

Color System:

Background: Deep black #0A0A0A
Primary accent: Brushed gold #B8924A with soft metallic luminosity — slightly glowing, never flat
Optional: Warm off-white #F5F0E8 for typographic contrast
Typography:

Wordmark: "PROXIMITY" in wide letter-spacing (tracking: luxury level)
Font style: Modern high-contrast serif OR clean geometric sans-serif
Feel: Refined, premium, European luxury brand energy
Lighting & Mood:

Cinematic — as if the logo is emerging from darkness
Soft inner glow on gold elements, subtle shadow depth
High contrast, no noise, no texture overload
Mood: Trustworthy, powerful, elegant, modern
VARIANT LIST TO GENERATE:

Variant 1 — Primary Lockup (Horizontal)
Monogram "P" symbol on the left + "PROXIMITY" wordmark to the right. Dark background. Full gold treatment. Used for website header and desktop banners.

Variant 2 — Stacked Lockup (Vertical)
Monogram "P" centered above the wordmark "PROXIMITY". Centered composition. Used for splash screens, loading screens, and presentations.

Variant 3 — App Icon / Favicon
The "P" monogram only, centered inside a deep black square with rounded corners. Gold glow effect. No wordmark. Optimized for small sizes — bold, recognizable, iconic.

Variant 4 — Light/Reversed Version
Same as Variant 1 or 2, but on a white or off-white #F5F0E8 background. Gold becomes slightly darker and more muted. Used for printed materials, email headers, and light-mode UI.

Variant 5 — Monochrome / Single Color
All-white or all-black version of the full lockup. No gold. Clean, flat. Used for embossing, watermarks, legal docs, or situations requiring one-color printing.

Variant 6 — Social Media Avatar
Circular crop version of the "P" monogram. Deep black circle, centered gold "P" with glow. Clean border or subtle ring in gold. Used for profile pictures across all platforms.

Variant 7 — Wordmark Only
"PROXIMITY" in the refined typeface, wide-spaced, gold on black — no symbol. Used for certain UI contexts, document headers, or brand moments where type alone leads.

Variant 8 — Cinematic Hero Presentation
Full brand presentation mockup — logo centered on a cinematic black canvas, soft volumetric light emanating behind the mark, slight lens flare or atmospheric haze. Used for website hero sections, investor decks, and brand reveals.

Output Specs for all variants:

Style: Ultra-clean, professional brand presentation
Format feel: As if designed by a top-tier brand agency
No watermarks, no mockup frames unless specified
